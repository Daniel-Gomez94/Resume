Project Two

Preserving Osteen’s Rural Character Through Strategic Planning

Description

This project developed a small area plan to preserve the rural identity of Osteen, FL. A professional planning role was taken to design land use, growth management, housing, placemaking, and transportation strategies, culminating in a written plan, presentation, and map.

Skills Learned

  • Urban and Regional Planning – Gained practical experience in designing land use and zoning strategies based on real-world planning challenges.
  • Policy Writing – Created actionable policy strategies including conservation easements, growth management boundaries, and zoning code proposals.
  • Supported map-based presentations and helped visualize planning boundaries like the GMB.
  • Sustainability and Resource Planning – Focused on aquifer recharge, stormwater management, and rural preservation.
  • Community Engagement Thinking – Designed public meeting strategies and considered stakeholder inclusion in policy implementation.
